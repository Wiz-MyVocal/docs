---
title: "Get Voices"
api: "GET https://test.myvocal.ai/sound_clone/api/v1/tts/voices"
description: "Return metadata about a specific voice."
---
### Header
<ParamField header="accessKey"   type="string" required>
  You can copy your api-key on https://test.myvocal.ai/profile after you have subscribed to Pro or Business Plan.
</ParamField>


### Response
<ResponseField name="code" type="number" required>
  Indicates whether the call was successful. 1 if successful.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="message" type="string" >
  When an exception occurs, specific exception information will be provided in the message.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="data" type="object" >
Includes specific data.
    <Expandable title="Toggle object">
        <ResponseField name="voices" type="list">
            All available voices under the current API-Key, including Premade voices provided by Myvocal, and Custom voices you created on the website and in the API.
            <Expandable title="Toggle object">
            <ResponseField name="voiceId" type="string" >
                Your voiceId
            </ResponseField>
            <ResponseField name="name" type="string" >
                Your voiceName
            </ResponseField>
            <ResponseField name="voiceType" type="string" >
                <div>cloned: create the voice by recording online or upload audio files.</div>
                <div>designed: create the voice by voice design</div>
            </ResponseField>
            <ResponseField name="voiceFeature" type="string" >
            Text-to-Speech or AI Cover
            </ResponseField>
            <ResponseField name="voiceGenerateSource" type="string" >
            web or api
            </ResponseField>
            </Expandable>
        </ResponseField>
    </Expandable>
</ResponseField>
